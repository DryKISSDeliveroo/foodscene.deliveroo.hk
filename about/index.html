About Deliveroo Foodscene

Deliveroo's on a mission to transform the way the world thinks about takeaway. We deliver amazing food from great restaurants – including major chains like sen-ryo and PizzaExpress, plus all your local favourites – straight to your door, fast.

We personally curate the restaurant selection in your local area, so we've taste-tested a lot of restaurants (and eaten a lot of food).

Here at Deliveroo Foodscene, we're sharing some of the best restaurant stories we've come across along the way. From interviews with top Hong Kong chefs to healthy breakfast inspiration, you'll find plenty to tickle your taste buds.

Want us to shout about what's cool in your city? Got a story we have to hear? Send foodie news / pictures to foodscene@deliveroo.co.hk.
