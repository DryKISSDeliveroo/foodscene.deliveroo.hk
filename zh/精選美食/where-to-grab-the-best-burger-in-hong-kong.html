在香港嚐到最好食的漢堡包

  • 22 September 2017
  • Ellie Tsui
  • 閱讀需時: 1 min
香港 深水埗 印度 漢堡包 啤酒 灣仔 串燒 銅鑼灣
分享此文章

漢堡包普遍被視為美式快餐嘅代表，多夾以牛肉為主（被認為正宗夾料），在配搭上配料和調味料就成為每家美式餐廳的招牌特色了。

4th of July at the Hive Studios, celebrating the origin of our Burger and America. #4thofjuly #america

A post shared by Burger Joys (@burgerjoys) on Jul 5, 2017 at 3:30am PDT

在香港，最早引入美式快餐-漢堡包的是麥當勞，他們早於1975年已在香港的銅鑼灣開業，令漢堡包開始為人熟悉和接受。

. I'm lovin' it🍔 #hongkong #muiwo #mcdonalds #mcdonaldshk #burger #love #m #bike #blackwhitethink #red #nikon #d3400 #hkig

A post shared by 黑•白想 (@black_whitethink) on Sep 11, 2017 at 10:40am PDT

發展到今日，漢堡包已經成為高級餐廳裏面可以品嚐到嘅菜式，由用嘅麵包的軟硬度，口感，到用的材料及醬汁都變得講究。

咁講咗咁耐，究竟邊度有好嘅漢堡包食呢？ 答案就係呢度！

1. The Butchers Club Burger

· and after all thats been said and done you're just the part of me i can't let go · #thebutchersclub · #carpediem · #weekendwonderings

A post shared by • CQ • (@leisikio) on Sep 5, 2017 at 5:10am PDT

The Butchers Club Burger 是一間曾經連續兩年得到米芝蓮街頭小食推介的餐廳，實力當然不同凡響。他們當中的"The Burger" 最受歡迎，調好味道的漢堡扒，加上芝士、煙肉、蕃茄、酸瓜和秘製醬汁，食落滋味無窮。

The butchers club burger with a side of duck fat fries . . . #thebutchersclubburger #hongkongeats #burger #hamburger #cheeseburger #foodporn #foodphotography #meltedcheese #comfortfood

A post shared by wanderingepicurean (@wanderingepicurean) on Sep 1, 2017 at 8:26am PDT

如果唔喜歡肉類，可以試試佢哋嘅魚。呢個Captain Ahab 選用紐西蘭鱈魚柳，配上他他醬和涼伴捲心菜一起吃，非常滋味。

Captain Ahab Burger: Panko-breaded sustainable New Zealand Ling fillet with homemade tartar sauce and tangy coleslaw at The Butchers Club Burger 🍔😋 @the.butchers.club @katekatiekatharine

A post shared by Hungry Hong Kong (@danielhungryhk) on Apr 21, 2017 at 3:46am PDT

魚和肉都唔鍾意？唔緊要呢度仲有素食漢堡，無論賣相定係口感都跟真嘅牛肉漢堡好相似。

ATTENTION if you love to eat! I found the BEST burger at The Butchers Club. It was a bacon double cheese with the best bun and juiciest patty AND it was vegetarian!!! OMG it was sooo good my taste buds died and went to heaven. The Green Common also has a vegan burger loaded with avocado. Both use Beyond Meats and are really good!
