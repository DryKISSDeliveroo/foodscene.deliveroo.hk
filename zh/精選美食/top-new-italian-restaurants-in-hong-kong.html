香港3間新意大利餐廳人氣之選

  • 29 September 2017
  • Ellie Tsui
  • 閱讀需時: 1 min
香港 意大利 西營盤 中環 荔枝角 薄餅 意粉 意大利飯
分享此文章

講起意大利這國家，大家可能馬上聯想到名貴跑車及可瘋狂購物。其實，意大利菜可算是世界三大料理美食之一，菜式豐富多樣，有我們熟悉的薄餅、意粉、玉棋、餃子及意大利飯（Risotto）。以下向大家推介3間近期新開餐廳作品嘗。

1. Second Street Comfort Food & Bar

A happy team equals happy customer, here we are to kick start another brilliant week serving yummy food! 😊👋✌️👨🏼‍🍳👩🏽‍🍳🌈 #secondstreethk #saiyingpunrestaurant #hkgastropub #hkigers #happyteam #restaurantlife

A post shared by Second Street Hong Kong (@secondstreethk) on Aug 7, 2017 at 5:54pm PDT

Second Street Comfort Food & Bar位於西營盤，提供香脆小食、薄餅、意粉、主菜及甜品。特別推介，大廚帶來一道菜色名為Baked Whole Sea Bass in Lemon Garlic and Homemade Breadcrumbs，烹調方法，先把香草釀滿魚肚，而魚身以香草麵包屑作覆蓋，然後烤焗。這種處理手法可把鱸魚的肉汁封鎖在內，帶來有皮脆內嫩口感。

Dreaming of this baked sea bass with pesto breadcrumb! 🤤🤤🐟🍴 #secondstreethk #hkfoodie #hkgoodeats #pestobreadcrumbs #bakedwholeseabass #saiyingpuneats #noservicecharge #西營盤美食 #相機食先是常識吧 #新餐廳推介 #無加一抵讚 #地中海式烤魚

A post shared by Second Street Hong Kong (@secondstreethk) on Aug 7, 2017 at 5:21am PDT

2. Ciao Chow - Italian Cafeteria

Our bar is equipped with 25 taps: 16 craft beers plus a selection of microbrews from Hong Kong, Japan, Italy, Germany, U.K., Ireland and the US. We are more than ready for the SEVENS. Are you? 🏈🏈 #sevens #rugby #rugby7s2016 #wheretheworldcomestoplay #hk7s #beer #microbrews #craftbeer #draft #hongkong #hongkongsevens #bar #lkf

A post shared by Ciao Chow - Italian Cafeteria (@ciaochow.hk) on Apr 8, 2016 at 2:11am PDT

Ciao Chow - Italian Cafeteria 本店位於中環，他們今年再下一城，於九龍塘又一城開分店。那處是採用半自助式服務，提供預設意粉或薄餅。別外，還有個人自選口味，可因應自己喜愛創出獨一無二的口味，玩味甚濃。薄餅值得一試，皮薄如紙，用上石爐烤製，香脆可口。

#ciao #pizza

別外，推介該店的Fregola con Frutti di Mare，小圓型意粉配上海鮮大蝦，青口，蜆肉及濃濃蕃茄醬汁，好味到不能言語。

3. Si Simply Italian

Come and enjoy aperitif at Si @si_simplyitalian. Our signature drink- a famous Italian Spritz made of prosecco and Aperol, wide range of wines, cocktails and soft drinks plus free canapés Monday to Sunday 17.30-19.30. It will be a great time to relax after work and before having delicious dinner. #d2placeTWO #Dplace #si #simplyitalian #italianrestaurant #food #wine #spritz #aperol #crodino #specialtreats #italy #852 #hk #sisimplyitalian #happyhour #aperitiv #itlaliancoffee #sirestaurant #canapès #Laichikok #cheungshawan #instafood #foodporn #followme #lifestyle #silifestyle

A post shared by Si Simply Italian (@si_simplyitalian) on Oct 27, 2016 at 1:52am PDT

Si Simply Italian位處荔枝角工廈內，食物質素高，連居港意大利人都喜歡，便可見一斑。除了有大家熟悉的risotto外，還提供貓耳朵麵，由於麵團分成一小片，再捲成小卷，狀似貓耳朵得名。好像Spreadable Spicy Salame便用上它作麵食，配上意大利辣豬肉腸醬，番茄，黑橄欖及羊奶芝士，味道非常之夾，令人滋味無窮！

Orecchiette #jovy_princess #d2place #hkfoodie #hkfoodblogger #hkig @si_simplyitalian #boxingdaylunch

A post shared by Jovy公主的華麗餐單 (@jovy_princess) on Dec 27, 2016 at 7:20am PST

可見同一種類美食也隱藏在各區，大家細味尋找，便得到發掘嘅樂趣！
