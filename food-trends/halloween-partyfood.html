Here’re The Foods You Need For An Unforgettable Halloween Party

  • 26 October 2017
  • Deliveroo Hong Kong
  • Read time: 3 min
pumpkin autumn halloween party food
Share this article

Halloween is just a few days away! We have hand-picked a list of popular restaurants for you to order the perfect foods to get the party started! Check out our recommendation below.

McDonald's (All branches)

When it comes to party food, McNuggets are a must-have. For a limited time only, try the newest speciality sauces, including honey mustard, Japanese style sweet curry sauce, Korean sweet & hot sauce and sesame miso sauce by ordering the 18pcs Chicken McNuggets® Combo. The nuggets will go out fast so secure yours as soon as they arrive!

Tai Tai Pie Pies（Admiralty）

Sweet or savory, no matter what your choice is, pie is always the answer. Be sure to order the in season Pumpkin & Spice Pie to satisfy your sweet tooth!

PizzaExpress（All branches）

A party without pizzas isn't a perfect party. PizzaExpress provides sets that serve over 20 people and require minimal hassles. All you need to do is send a group text to ask what types of pizza your friends want and schedule your order 2 hours in advance!

Cali-Mex (All branches)

Did you realize this year's Halloween is on a tuesday? If you haven't made up your mind yet, order up a big portion of delicious tacos, just because it's #TacoTuesday, and taco makes everyone happy.

OPPA Chicken (Sai Ying Pun)

One does not simply eat only one piece of fried chicken. Impress your guests with some crispy and juicy Korean fried chicken!

Start prepare your party by ordering via our app or website now!
