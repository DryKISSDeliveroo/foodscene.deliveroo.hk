Deliveroo for Business - Referral Campaign

Refer a company and get a HK$300 voucher,

  1. This referral offer (the Referral Offer) is made by Deliveroo Hong Kong Ltd ("Deliveroo for Business") open to residents of Hong Kong aged 18 or over.
  1. To participate in this Referral Offer, you must complete and submit one of our referral forms with details including the name of a company that has not already signed up to Deliveroo for Business, and a decision maker within that company, before the Closing Date. If the company you refer meets our criteria to sign up for Deliveroo for Business, signs up, and then spends at least £50 within the first four weeks of signing up, we will apply HK$300 credit to your personal Deliveroo account.
  1. If more than one successful referral is received by Deliveroo for Business in respect of any company, only one person will receive the credit - and that person will be determined by Deliveroo for Business based on (1) whether the decision maker referred is the person who engages with Deliveroo for Business; and (2) which person submitted the referral form the earliest.  
  1. Deliveroo credit is subject to the usual order conditions and Deliveroo's Voucher Terms and Conditions and any minimum spend restrictions communicated to you. Credits must be redeemed within three calendar months. There will be a processing time of up to seven days after the date of a successful referral before credits will be applied to your account. Credits have no cash value and are non-transferable.
  1. Before you submit the name and email of anyone to us, please ensure that you have obtained their permission. We will use the personal details of the colleague supplied for the administration of this promotion and introductory emails about Deliveroo for Business products and services. Please see our main privacy policy.
  1. Deliveroo for Business reserves the right to amend or suspend the availability of this offer at any time.
  1. Deliveroo Hong Kong Ltd is registered in Hong Kong with a company number 2288695, and a registered office at 21st Floor, Nam Wo Hong Building, 148 Wing Lok Street, Hong Kong.

  1. 如欲參加本推薦優惠，閣下必須在截止日期前填妥及遞交一份推薦表格，提供資料包括仍未加入Deliveroo企業版的公司及該公司決策人之名稱。如閣下推薦的公司符合加入Deliveroo企業版的準則、加入Deliveroo企業版，並於加入Deliveroo企業版起首四星期內消費最少50歐元，Deliveroo企業版將於閣下的個人Deliveroo戶口入賬300港元。
  1. 如Deliveroo企業版就任何公司收到多於一份成功推薦，則只有一人會獲入賬，Deliveroo企業版將根據(1)所推薦的決策人是否加入Deliveroo企業版的人士；及(2)最早提交推薦表格之人士來決定獲入賬的人士。
  1. 入賬受一般訂購情況、Deliveroo優惠劵條款及條件及任何已通知閣下的最低消費要求所限制。入賬必須於三個曆月內兌換。入賬至閣下戶口的所需辦理時間為成功推薦日期的七天內。入賬沒有現金價值及不得轉讓。
  1. 在閣下向Deliveroo企業版提交任何人士之姓名及電郵前，請確保閣下已取得該人士的許可。Deliveroo企業版將使用所提供的加盟人士之個人資料，作管理本推廣及Deliveroo企業版之產品及服務介紹電郵的用途。請參考Deliveroo企業版的主要私隱政策。
