Delicious! New Restaurants On Deliveroo

  • 18 September 2017
  • Deliveroo Hong Kong
  • Read time: 3 min
new restaurants gordon ramsay promotion
Share this article

Have you ever dreamed of enjoying an incredible steak, sipping a nice glass of wine and finishing the dinner with a satisfying toffee pudding, all at your own dining room?

This week, an amazing list of restaurants is partnering up with Deliveroo. From classic cheeseburgers to creative fusion dishes, enjoy amazing food at home in just a few clicks! Check out the latest restaurants roster now:

Olive (Central)

Greek & Middle Eastern

A post shared by Olive, Greek & Middle Eastern (@olivehongkong) on Jul 21, 2017 at 2:03am PDT

Maybe you couldn't go on a vacation in the Mediterranean straight away, but these exotic and refreshing dishes from Olive will for sure bring you the Aegean Sea vibes that you need after a busy day.

Bombay Dreams (Sheung Wan)

Indian

A post shared by Bombay Dreams (@bombaydreamshk) on Sep 1, 2017 at 2:52am PDT

Who could resist a masala and naan combo, seriously? Try out their authentic and mouth-burning curries tonight!

Mama San (Central)

South East Asian

A post shared by Mama San Hong Kong (@mamasanhongkong) on Aug 14, 2017 at 11:55pm PDT

Mama San truly understands the meaning of "When East Meets West". Explore a wide range of South East Asian menu with a hint of Western influence.

Bouchon (Central)

French

A post shared by Bouchon Bistro Français (@bouchonbistrohk) on Aug 7, 2017 at 11:29pm PDT

Impress your better half with these iconic Parisian cuisines and fine French wine. All you need to prepare is some nice candles at home!

Soho Spice (Central)

South East Asian

A post shared by Soho Spice (@sohospicehk) on Jul 5, 2017 at 8:55pm PDT

Spice things up with these street food inspired dishes and finish it off with some satisfying desserts such as Nutella Roti and Mango Sticky Rice.

Craftsteak (Central)

Western

A post shared by Craftsteak Open Fire Cooking (@craftsteakhk) on Aug 18, 2017 at 2:06am PDT

Juicy, perfectly cut steaks from Australia, USA and Canada come with phenomenal sides including mac & cheese, mash, creamed spinach, you name it. Need us to say more?

London House by Gordon Ramsay (Tsim Sha Tsui)

British

A post shared by London House (@londonhousehk) on Mar 23, 2017 at 5:27pm PDT

There's plenty of classic British food to find from this celebrity chef's restaurants. Shepard's Pie, Bangers & Mash, Fish & Chips. You name it, we've got it.

Tango (Central & Tsim Sha Tsui)

Argentinean

A post shared by Tango Argentinian Steak House (@tangohongkong) on Aug 29, 2017 at 12:58am PDT

Did you know that beef is a key component of traditional Argentine cuisine? Order these juicy, pink in center steaks done in Argentinian style for a perfecto dinner date.

Le Pain Quotidien (Admiralty, Wanchai & West Kowloon)

Western

A post shared by Le Pain Quotidien Hong Kong (@lepainquotidienhk) on Aug 18, 2017 at 1:52am PDT

Kick start your day with some freshly-baked baguettes and homemade Belgian waffles! Monday Blues? What's that?

Nahm Vietnamese & Thai (West Kowloon)

Vietnamese & Thai

A post shared by Nahm, Vietnamese & Thai (@nahmhongkong) on Aug 9, 2017 at 8:00pm PDT

Craving for something spicy and rich in flavor? Nahm's chicken satay, pad thai and wok braised beef are for sure the dishes you don't want to miss!
