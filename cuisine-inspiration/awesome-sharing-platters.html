Share the love: 7 awesome sharing platters

  • 10 July 2017
  • Editor
  • Read time: 4 min
sharing poke sushi burrito
Share this article

Sharing platters make the perfect takeaway. Not only do they allow you to try all sorts of different goodies at once, and whether it's friends, family or work clients, they bring everyone together at the dinner table. So if you've got company, and you want to make sure they leave full and happy, here's our a few of our favourite platters in Hong Kong.

1. Set for 15, POKE

A post shared by P.O.K.E. (@poke.hk) on Feb 5, 2017 at 8:02pm PST

If you'd like a taste of the sun-kissed shores of Hawaii, try ordering from POKE. You'll find this classic fish salad dish on their set menus which are perfect when ordered as platters. The collection here feeds 15 people, and offers up the best of their menu. You'll find everything from their gourmet poke bowls to a hearty King Crab Salad in this wonder.

Where: POKE, Caroline Hill

2. Party Set, Yung Kee Restaurant

For traditional Cantonese eats, try the platters over at Yung Kee Restaurant – you can order them in sets of 6, 12 or 25, which means you can cater to everything from a get-together with friends to an office away-day. Our favourite dish is their incredible, aromatic charcoal-roasted goose, which thankfully comes as part of all their platters.

Where: Yung Kee Restaurant, Central

3. San Francisco, Cali-Mex

A post shared by Cali-Mex (@calimexbarngrill) on Nov 23, 2016 at 10:09pm PST

With Mexican food famous for handy street treats like burritos, empanadas and tacos, it's no surprise that when they come together, it's truly awesome. And Cali-Mex is used to catering for groups, so you'll find an extensive platter menu on offer here. If you're ordering for a few friends, their Junk Boat Packages have everything from spicy burritos and bursting tacos to cheesy quesadillas and smoky chipotle pulled pork.

Where: Cali-Mex, various locations

4. I Knead Lunch 10 Person, Knead

A post shared by Knead HK (@kneadhk) on Apr 25, 2017 at 8:20pm PDT

If you're looking for light lunch platters, Knead is the ideal place for your order. They have a huge variety of Catering Sandwiches which can be freshly prepared in large batches to the highest standard – whether it's their tender braised beef brisket, salty Parma ham or smoked salmon, you'll be spoilt for choice with fillings.

Where: Knead, locations in Quarry Bay and Sheung Wan

5. For 5 Pax, Fishsteria Seafood Place

A post shared by Fishsteria (@fishsteria_hk) on Sep 28, 2016 at 2:02am PDT

As you can probably guess from the name, the chefs at Fishsteria specialise in seafood, ideal for corporate platters where you want to impress, especially when there's whole, fresh lobster on the menu. Opt for the For 5 Pax – with marinated Venetian sardines, crab meat burrata salads, a whole lobster and more – or go for the 10 or 20 person option depending on how many taste buds you've got to wow.

Where: Fishsteria Seafood Place, Wanchai

6. Set For 10, The Butchers Club Burger

A post shared by Deliveroo 戶戶送 (@deliveroo_hk) on Feb 1, 2016 at 5:30pm PST

If you're looking to serve up a gourmet barbecue to your guests, try the Butcher's Club. Serving up American grilled recipes all day long, their party set platters are bursting with chargrilled specialities, and their sliders are sure to be a hit with your whole party. This delight serves 10, so you're all set for your next get together.

Where: The Butchers Club Burger, various locations

7. Joe's Deluxe, Kyoto Joe

A post shared by KyotoJoe (@kyotojoelkf) on Jul 8, 2017 at 1:18am PDT

Bitesize and with so many varieties, sushi is the perfect platter food. At Kyoto Joe's, you can mix and match your sashimi for a tailored order, or keep things simple with one of their combo meals. Our favourite is Joe's Deluxe, which includes chopped scallop, salmon, tuna, yellowfish and sweet shrimp.

Where: Kyoto Joe, Soho

Whether you've got family and friends coming around, or clients you need to impress, there are dozens of sharing platter styles on Deliveroo.
