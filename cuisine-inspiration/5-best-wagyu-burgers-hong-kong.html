5 of the best wagyu burgers you can get your hands on in Hong Kong

You'll find burgers topping off menus across the world, but not all are created equal. Wagyu burgers are practically burger royalty. And this is because of a thin marbled layer of fat throughout that melts when cooked, giving each patty a deeper flavour. So when the time calls for a proper burger, we've rounded up a few of our favourites for you to check out.
