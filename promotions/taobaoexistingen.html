From Thursday, 7th March 2019 to Saturday, 9th March 2019, get the best of both worlds from Deliveroo and Taobao's International Women's Day offer!

Enjoy a discount of ¥25 for all orders valued ¥200 or above by entering promo code "deliveroo" before checkout. Offer is valid to existing Taobao users only. Terms and conditions applied.

What are you waiting for? Celebrate International Women's Day rewarding yourself with some good food and a shopping spree here now!
