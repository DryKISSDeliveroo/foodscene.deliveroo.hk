Exclusive offer 1 | Deliveroo x Bank of China Mastercard: $50 off your Deliveroo order!

From now until May 31, 2018, Bank of China Mastercard cardholders can enjoy HK$50 discount when ordering HK$250 or more (excluding delivery fee) from deliveroo.hk or mobile app by inputting promo code "BOCMC+ first 4 digits of your BOC Credit Card Number" before checkout and pay with Bank of China Mastercard card.

This offer is limited to the first 1,000 Cardholders on a first-come-first-served basis. Each cardholder can enjoy this offer once only. This offer will be expired upon 14 days of the redemption of this offer.

May 2018 promo code: MC18MAY
