Mastercard Credit Card Exclusive Offers

  • 27 June 2018
  • Deliveroo Hong Kong
  • Read time: 7 min

Share this article

Pay securely with Mastercard on Deliveroo!

Exclusive offer 1 | First-time Deliveroo customers can enjoy 3 free deliveries

From now until December 31, 2018, first-time Deliveroo members (First-time offer is only valid for users who have never ordered from deliveroo before) who set any Mastercard Credit Card as the default payment card during registration can enjoy free delivery 3 times* by inputting promo code "MC2018NEW" before checkout.

Exclusive offer 2 | Existing Deliveroo customers can enjoy HK$30 off HK$200 twice each month

From now until December 31, 2018, existing Deliveroo customers can enjoy HK$30 discount twice each month on orders of HK$200 or more (excluding delivery fees)^. All you need to do is apply the corresponding promo code for each month before checkout and pay with your Mastercard credit card at deliveroo.hk or on Deliveroo's mobile app. This offer is only applicable to the first 4,200 cardholders every month, while stocks last.

  • July 2018: MC18JUL
  • August 2018: MC18AUG
  • September 2018: MC18SEP
  • October 2018: MC18OCT
  • November 2018: MC18NOV
  • December 2018: MC18DEC

*Terms and Conditions for First-time Deliveroo customers HK$90 discount:

  1. Deliveroo Offer ("Offer") is only applicable to the cardholders ("Cardholders") holding Mastercard credit cards ("Eligible Credit Cards"). Customers holding any of the Eligible Credit Cards shall be referred to as the "Cardholders" below. An Eligible Credit Card must be used for all relevant payments upon placing food orders ("Eligible Transactions") during the Promotion Period in order to enjoy any of the offers under this promotion.
  2. The "Promotion Period" of the Offer runs from 6:00a.m. 1 July 2018 to 11:59 p.m. 31 Dec 2018, time refers to Hong Kong time.
  3. Cardholders conducting Eligible Transactions on the Deliveroo official website (www.deliveroo.hk) or mobile application with Eligible Credit Cards during the Promotion Period shall be entitled to the following Offers:
  • Offer 1: Upon successful registration of a Deliveroo account and enters his/ her Eligible Credit Card as the default payment card on or before 31 December 2018, he/ she can apply the code "MC2018NEW" before checkout on the first order to enjoy free delivery for 3 times within 60 days after applying such promo code. Promo Code only needs to be entered once and is good for 3 uses.
  • Offer 2: For existing Deliveroo members, apply the promo code for the corresponding calendar month of July to December as stated below before checkout to enjoy HK$30 off two times when making each net order of HK$200 or above (excluding delivery fee) during Promotion Period.  The Offer is limited to the first 4,200 eligible Mastercard Cardholders per calendar month:
  • Promo code for July 2018: MC18JUL
  • Promo code for August 2018: MC18AUG
  • Promo code for September 2018: MC18SEP
  • Promo code for October 2018: MC18OCT
  • Promo code for November 2018: MC18NOV
  • Promo code for December 2018: MC18DEC

Cardholder only needs to apply the relevant promo code once; thereafter, applicable free delivery offer will be deducted for such Eligible Transaction, and the remaining of the unclaimed free delivery will be credited to the Cardholder's Deliveroo membership account, and will be applied to subsequent Eligible Transactions until fully claimed by Cardholder. After applying the promo code, Cardholder has to claim the discount within 60 days, otherwise unclaimed discount will be forfeited.

Offer is available on a first come first served basis. Credits that go with the Offer are valid for 30 days upon redemption.
