Exclusive offer | Deliveroo x DBS Mastercard: HK$25 off two times every month till May!

From now until Sunday, May 10, 2020, DBS Mastercard cardholders can enjoy HK$25 discount twice on orders of HK$250 or more (excluding delivery fees)* from deliveroo.hk or mobile app by inputting promo code "DBSHKMC+ first 6 digits of your DBS Credit Card Number" before checkout and pay with DBS Mastercard card. This offer is limited to the first 7,200 eligible transactions, while stocks last so hurry up!.

*T&Cs apply.

Order now!
