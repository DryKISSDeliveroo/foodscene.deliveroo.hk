Special Christmas with Roo

  • 1 December 2017
  • Deliveroo Hong Kong
  • Read time: 1 min
promotion christmas
Share this article

It's beginning to look a lot like Christmas…! As Christmas is all about giving, on that cold December night, invite your loved ones over and treat them to your favorite dishes from Deliveroo. We have teamed up with 10 restaurants partners to bring you free "Coca-Cola"! Starting from today till 31st December 2017, enjoy a complimentary can of "Coca-Cola" / "Coca-Cola Zero" (330ml) for every designated dish* when you order from the selected restaurants below. The more you order, the more you'll be rewarded!

Free "Coca-Cola":

The Boathouse (Stanley)

Beerliner (Mong Kok, Olympian City, Whampoa Garden, Kwai Fong)

Cafe Deco Pizzeria (Elements, Sheung Wan)

FAUCHON Paris Le Café (Sha Tin)

Made in HK (Kwun Tong)

Mango Tree (Elements)

Mango Tree Cafe (Cityplaza)

Peak Cafe Bar (Olympian City)

Pickled Pelican (Stanley)

ZERO (Mong Kok)**

All we want for Christmas is Roo! Happy ordering!

*Applicable to certain dishes only. Available while stocks last.

**You will receive a complimentary can of "Coca-Cola Zero" instead of "Coca-Cola".

"Coca-Cola" and "Coca-Cola Zero" are registered trademarks of The Coca-Cola Company.
