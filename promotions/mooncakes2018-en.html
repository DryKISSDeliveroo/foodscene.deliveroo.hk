Mooncake Pre-order and Delivery Now Available!

  • 6 September 2018
  • June Leung
  • Read time: 3 min
promotion mooncake mid-autumn preorder
Share this article

Mid-Autumn Festival is just around the corner this month! Haven't bought your fair share of moon cakes yet? No worries, have them Deliveroo-ed to your doorstep! Look out for the "Mooncake Special" menu tag to find out which restaurants offer mooncakes.

Here are our top 7 hand-picked favorites:

Duddells

Savour Duddells' signature silky smooth mooncakes in regular and mini sized delights!

Available for order and delivery in Central.

Kee Wah Bakery

Enjoy an exclusive 10% off on the smoothest egg custard mooncakes presented as ethereal rabbits.

Available for order and delivery in Wan Chai, Kwai Chung and Admiralty.

Island Shangri-la

Summer Palace's Michelin chef designed mini red bean paste mooncakes with 60-year preserved tangerine peel.

Available for order and delivery in Admiralty.

L'hotel

Enjoy Mini High Fiber Mooncake with an exclusive 20% off on pre-orders before Sept 8th.

Available for order and delivery at all branches.

Chef Nic

Exclusive Cha Chaan Teng themed Mid-Autumn Delights by celebrity Nicholas Tse.

Available for order and delivery in Central.

XTC Gelato

Presenting Chocolate, Crispy Chocolate Drop, Hong Kong Milk Tea and Strawberry mooncakes.

Available for order and delivery in Central and Tsim Sha Tsui.

Fung Shing

Share tasty traditional Double Egg Yolk & Lotus Seed Paste mooncakes with family and friends.

Available for order and delivery in Sheung Wan.
